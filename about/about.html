Short Breif About Me

Makeup has always inspired me, I remember as a little girl I always came across people who'd discourage girl's from wearing colorful or vibrant makeup. I loved how a mascara could make my eyes look more expressive or how a vibrant lip color could make me feel stronger. How often is a bold red lip seen as too bossy and fierce and how bright color's are a major NO-NO for chocolaty skin tones. My vision has been inspired by Marilyn Monroe, I've always been amazed at how she owned herself as a fashion icon and ruled over everyone's heart with nothing more than a solid red lip and the perfect liner. I am Ayesha Zia, and for me makeup has always been about breaking rules and stereotypes! I'm a certified makeup artist who hopes to play a small part in changing the conventional beauty standards of our society.
